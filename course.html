Degree

Undergraduate education is education conducted after secondary education and before postgraduate education. It typically includes all postsecondary programs up to the level of a bachelor's degree.

Post-Graduation

Postgraduate education involves learning and studying for academic or professional degrees, academic or professional certificates, academic or professional diplomas, or other qualifications for which a first or bachelor's degree generally is required, and it is normally considered to be part of higher education.

Our Facilities

The Campus is endowed with renovated hostels, medical support systems (along with physiotherapy centre), modern gymnasium, swimming pool, a huge parking facility (available to both students and faculty), air conditioned sports complex and playground of international standards. A complete smoke and vehicle free zone, the College provides an eco-friendly environment to its students with the latest initiatives of solar heating, rainwater harvesting, paper recycling and other green initiatives. The College has taken measures to address the need for vigilance and security. The campus is CCTV-enabled to cater to the safety requirements of a well endowed and equipped campus receiving a huge footfall.

World Class Library

Attached to the main academic block, the Library is a two storey centrally air-conditioned and Wi-Fi enabled unit with seating capacity for about 300 readers divided between two floors.

Tasty And Healthy Food

The College Canteen is a popular meeting place for students serving a variety of dishes at moderate prices. The ambience of the College canteen reflects the youthfulness of its visitors with its elegant tables, cozy chairs and colorful grafitti.

Largest Playground

The College has a state-of-art international standard multipurpose indoor sports stadium. The courts laid with wooden planks are used for Badminton, Basket Ball, Volley Ball, Judo, Wrestling, etc.
